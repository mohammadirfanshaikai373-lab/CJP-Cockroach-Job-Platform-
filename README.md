CJP Connect – Grow, Learn, & Get Hired

CJP Connect is a modern professional network built for Gen‑Z job seekers, mentors, and early‑career professionals.
Move beyond static profiles: connect through help‑based posts, skill commitments, and real‑time mentorship.

Why CJP Connect?
✅ Help‑Centric Feed – Ask for help or offer guidance. Posts tagged by skill, urgency, and commitment type (15‑min call, file review, shadow task).
✅ Smart Job Board – Discover entry‑level and internship roles. Save jobs, apply directly, and contact recruiters.
✅ Mentor Matching – Connect with industry mentors who review your projects, give career advice, and vouch for your skills.
✅ Reputation System – Earn points by completing commitments, giving thanks, and helping others. Unlock badges and climb the leaderboard.
✅ Project Proofs – Showcase your work with GitHub links, Loom videos, or case studies. Prove what you built and what you learned.
✅ Private Messaging – Chat one‑on‑one, share files, and turn conversations into paid or volunteer commitments.
✅ Resume Builder – Create, edit, and export modern‑style resumes directly from your profile.
✅ Events & Challenges – Join company‑hosted coding challenges and virtual workshops. Win prizes and skip HR rounds.
✅ Dark Mode & Privacy – Choose anonymous mode for sensitive questions, control who sees your profile.

Perfect for:

Students & fresh graduates looking for first jobs.

Mentors wanting to give back and earn recognition.

Companies that want to discover hidden talent through challenges.

Join the community that helps you grow, one commitment at a time.
